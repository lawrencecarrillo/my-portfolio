About Me

From Puppets to Pixels

Originally from California, I've called New York City home for 19 years. The energy of this city fuels my diverse passions and endless curiosity.

I'm currently studying at Pursuit's AI Native Program in Long Island City — building real technical skills and diving deep into the world of Artificial Intelligence.

With a background spanning acting, puppeteering, restaurant management, and customer service, I bring adaptability and dependability to every environment I step into. I've always been someone who figures it out — no matter the challenge.

Creative at my core, I can't wait to collaborate and build with AI. After years of making puppets come to life with my bare hands, I'm now applying that same creativity to the frontiers of technology.
